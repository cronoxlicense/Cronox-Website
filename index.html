Can I Buy?

Official licenses can be safely purchased, only through our Cronox website and the authorized resellers. The website will let you pay with Paypal / IDPAY / cryptocurrency for now You can find our official resellers only on the Cronox discord for Other Payment Method.

How Can i Activate License ?

Website: When your payment is done, do a screenshot of your Order ID, and open a ticket on Cronox discord, and wait for the activation by our staff. Reseller: If you're buying via an authorized reseller, your license will be activated by him.

DISCLAIMER

Scammers try to impersonate resellers and official staff! Check the names and links if they're authentic or not. Always check if the website link or the username contains special characters, trying to mimic the real ones. The real authorized Cronox resellers and staff, are ONLY inside the official discord.
